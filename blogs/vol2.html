Highlights of the Week

Welcome back! The Stitch Studio team has been working hard on all fronts to push the project forward. Let me show you some highlights from the past week!

On the software side, we started working on the stepper motor controller and the piece that parses and sends g-code commands from the laptop to the machine. On the mechanical side, Yaning and Rachel fabricated the stepper motor mounts for the base plate as well as the two other acrylic plates.
